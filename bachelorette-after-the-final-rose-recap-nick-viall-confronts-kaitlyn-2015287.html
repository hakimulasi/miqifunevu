Bachelorette After the Final Rose Recap: Nick Viall Confronts Kaitlyn

July 19, 2024
blog

Shawnlyn forever! Kaitlyn Bristowe accepted Shawn Booth's proposal on ABC's The Bachelorette, which means it was time another After the Final Rose special. Kaitlyn was forced to rehash her relationship with runner-up, Nick Viall, and things got awkward pretty quickly — hardly a surprise given that this was Nick's second time losing The Bachelorette.

Check out highlights from Kaitlyn and Nick's confrontation below, plus Nick's epic face-to-face with Shawn.

Nick Comes Clean: I Was Immature

Nick and Shawn were bitter enemies during The Bachelorette, but Nick was ready to bury the hatchet with his rival. "I'll say this about the whole Shawn thing. At the end of the day it was just pretty silly and unfortunate," he told host Chris Harrison.

Nick said he regretted his actions while watching the season: "I let those frustrations get the best of me," he told Harrison. "I ended up not being much better. We let our immaturity come out; we weren't the best versions of ourselves."

Nick and Shawn Kiss and Makeup (Or, Sit Awkwardly Next To Each Other)

Good news, everyone: Chris Harrison encouraged Nick and Shawn to work through their issues, and they kind of made up. At first, Shawn went on the defense, saying, "I can't try to be nice to somebody I don't have a good feeling about," but he eventually agreed to move on. In fact, Shawn accused ABC of over-dramatizing his fight with Nick.

"There were so many moments that weren't shown because they wanted to get this big feud between us," he said. "It's kinda disappointing."

Nick Confronts Kaitlyn: "We Said 'I Love You'"

Remember when Kaitlyn rejected Nick mid-proposal? Well, Nick told her exactly how he felt during ATFR: "We said 'I love you' to each other," he explained. "That was towards the end, and obviously I took that to heart. I'm wondering what you were thinking."

Kaitlyn wasn't thrilled by Nick's question, responding (with an epic eye-roll), "That's what happens in this world. You get into situations. I think at the end of the day, no love that I had was the one that I had for Shawn. I think that is the only explanation."

Apparently, Kaitlyn Stole Nick's Perfect Proposal

The most awkward moment of the night? When Chris Harrison made Kaitlyn and Nick watch his failed proposal in front of a live audience. Nick couldn't control his emotions, and lashed out at Kaitlyn: "Those words I said to you, I'll never have that back. I don't understand what the heck you were thinking, thinking 'There's no good way, but this is the best way to break his heart.'"

Kaitlyn kind of apologized to Nick, but he wasn't having it. "I wish that there was more consideration for what we had, or more importantly how it would affect me," he said.

Shawn Is Ready To Stand By His Woman
