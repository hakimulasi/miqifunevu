Early Beginnings

Tamar Halperin, born in the vibrant city of Tel Aviv in 1976, embarked on her musical journey after an initial stint as a tennis player.

Academic Pursuits

Tamar’s academic journey is notable, culminating in a PhD from the renowned Juilliard School in New York City, focusing on the works of Johann Sebastian Bach. Her education laid a robust foundation for her diverse musical career, blending classical elements with modern expressions.

Throughout her career, Tamar Halperin has demonstrated versatility across various music genres, including Baroque, classical, and jazz. She is not only a skilled harpsichordist but also a talented pianist and musicologist, making significant contributions to each field.
