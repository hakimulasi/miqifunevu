Raju Srivastava, a name synonymous with comedy in India, embarked on his journey to fame from the humble beginnings in Kanpur. His early life was marked by a passion for mimicry and entertainment, which propelled him to move to Mumbai, the city of dreams, in the 1980s.

His career, spanning over three decades, was a testament to his versatility and talent.

The Rise of a Comedy King

Raju’s breakthrough came with his participation in The Great Indian Laughter Challenge, where he didn’t just participate but left an indelible mark as the runner-up. This platform catapulted him to national fame, earning him the title of The King of Comedy.
