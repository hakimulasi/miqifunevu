Lisa Rinna’s Career Beginnings and Early Earnings

Lisa Rinna’s journey to fame began in Newport Beach, California. After moving to Medford, Oregon at the age of seven, she completed her high school education and moved to San Francisco to pursue modeling.

This career path soon led to TV commercials, setting the stage for her entry into acting.

Her breakthrough came in 1992 when she was cast as Billie Reed on Days of Our Lives, a role that brought her national recognition and considerable earnings.
