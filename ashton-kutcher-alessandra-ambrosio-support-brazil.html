Ashton Kutcher in Brazil for shocking World Cup match as Adriana and Alessandra are left disappointe

September 10, 2024
blog

    Ashton Kutcher headed down to Brazil for the World Cup this week – and he picked quite a match to watch. The actor was among those in the Estádio Mineirão stadium as home nation Brazil were crushed by Germany in a historic 7-1 defeat in the semi-final stages.

    Ahead of the game, Ashton shared a selfie showing him smiling alongside a friend, who was dressed in patriotic yellow and green. Wisely, in hindsight, Ashton chose not to show his allegiance and was dressed in neutral colours. "WorldCup2014," Ashton, 36, captioned the snapshot, captured moments before the match got underway. Understandably absent on the night was the star's pregnant fiancée Mila Kunis. The actress was no doubt resting back at home in LA following the couple's visit to Italy at the weekend to celebrate the wedding of Google's Nikesh Aror and entrepreneur Ayesha Thapar.

    Monday's shocking World Cup clash in the city of Belo Horizonte was watched by millions around the world, including Brazil's supermodel supporters Alessandra Ambrosio and Adriana Lima.The two Victoria's Secret stars got into their yellow and green jerseys to cheer on their team at Miss Lily's bar In New York's East Village. Adriana proudly sported her top, which had the number two and name 'Lims' on the back, while Alessandra's read 'Ale' under the number ten. The pair were also joined by fellow model Lily Aldridge to watch the nail-biting game and shared a number of photos throughout the day.

    But, for all their support, the stars were left bitterly disappointed by the time the final whistle went – unlike Heidi Klum. The German beauty shared a picture on her Instagram showing her standing infront of a huge TV screen with her hands over her eyes. "Can you believe this… I can't. 1…2…3…4…5…6…7…," she wrote alongside the photo.
