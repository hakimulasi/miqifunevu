Hunter Biden is an American investment advisor and lawyer who is a founding partner of Rosemont Seneca Partners, an investment and advisory firm. He is also widely recognized as the second son of the United States President, Joe Biden. Joe previously served as the 47th vice president during the Obama presidency, He defeated former president Donald Trump in the 2020 presidential election.

Apart from his father's name and fame, Hunter has been quite successful in his career. He was previously recruited by the board of Burisma Holdings which ended up in April 2019. In 2006, he was even appointed to a five-year term on the board of directors by President George W. Bush in Amtrak but he later resigned in February 2009.

Furthermore, federal authorities are actively investigating the business dealings of Hunter and the anti-corruption efforts of fathers during the time Joe was vice president.

Blessed with one son with wife, Melissa Cohen

The successful Biden's son, Hunter is popular with his both personal and professional life. In 2018, he met South African filmmaker, Melissa Cohen. After the first good impression, the couple started hanging out together and began a new relationship. In May 2019 after dating for almost a year the couple accepted one another as husband and wife. 

Many high-profile guests were seen in the grand ceremony, especially numerous political giants greet the newlywed couple. In March 2020 the pair welcomed their first child, son, in Los Angeles. As of now, the family of three are enjoying happy and prosperous living.

Divorced, Affairs, and Children

Hunter has been one of the highlighted personalities with his professional life and personal life. In 1993, he married his first wife, Kathleen Buhle which he first met back in 1990. However, after twenty-two years of their marriage, the couple announced their separation in 2015. Later, they officially divorced in 2017. 

Despite their separation, they are still parents of three beautiful children. Naomi Biden, Maisy Biden, and Finnegan Biden. As of now, the three kids are living with their single mother and Hunter often visits them. Furthermore, he is also the father of Lunden Alexis Robert's child who was born in August 2018.

Dated brother, Beau Biden's widow, Hallie Biden

In 2016, Hunter and his brother, Beau's widow, Hallie Biden shocked the world with the news of their relationship. They became a matter of gossip after their dating news broke out. 

Hunter even stated that 'he and Hallie are very much lucky to have found each other in this difficult time.' However, after enjoying one year of their relationship the couple broke up in 2017 and choose a separate path.

Net worth in Millions

Back in 1996, he served for the Delaware-based bank MBNA where he used to earn over $100,000. Similarly, he was paid an impressive amount of salary of $1.2 Million while serving at Paradigm Global Advisors. Furthermore, his father, Joe has a net worth of $9 Million as of 2020.

Bio

Hunter was born on 4th February 1970 in Wilmington, Delaware, United States. He graduated from Georgetown University and gathered a Juris Doctor degree from Yale University.
