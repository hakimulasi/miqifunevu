Kanye West and Wiz Khalifas Twitter Feud: See the Best Memes

September 02, 2024
blog

We will forever rethink responding “KK” in texts. Kanye West ranted at Wiz Khalifa on Twitter on Wednesday, January 27, after Khalifa commented about Yeezy changing the title of his upcoming album. Their feud resulted in more than 30 tweets from West — which he later deleted because of world peace, yo — and Khalifa’s Wikipedia page being edited to say he died from Twitter assault. 

The “See You Again” rapper, 28, felt that West changing his album title from Swish to Waves dishonors rapper Max B. “Hit this kk and become yourself,” Khalifa tweeted at the designer, referring to his reportedly relaxing strain of cannabis, Khalifa Kush.

But West, 38, took “kk” to be his wife Kim Kardashian’s initials, and that’s when things really took off. The “Facts” rapper then tweeted at Khalfa, telling him his music was “corny” and that Khalifa let a stripper — ex-wife Amber Rose — “trap” him. West then added that he initially looked at Khaifa’s Twitter account because he was “wearing cool pants.”

“I screen grabbed those pants and sent it to my style team #Wizwearscoolpants,” West tweeted at Khalifa, blessing the Internet with a now viral hashtag and meme.

Rose even weighed in with her own inappropriate memo regarding a particular body part of West’s — and the Yeezy designer ended up deleting his rant, claiming he just wants “positive energy blessings.”
