Fans of Counting On are now wondering whether or not Jinger Duggar is expecting once again, only six months after she gave birth to her and Jeremy Vuolo’s firstborn! The speculations started from a picture that the husband posted.

The snap shows the reality TV star all cuddled up on a comfy armchair with a hot drink in hand and reading a book.

‘She loves cozy winter evenings with a good book and a cup of tea,’ the hubby wrote in the caption.
