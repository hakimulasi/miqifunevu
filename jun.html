Jun is a South Korean singer, musician, dancer, actor, reality television personality, and performer who has served as the leader, lead vocalist, and lead dancer of the South Korean boy band A.C.E. The band’s discography consists of albums, extended plays, reissues, and tracks such as All I Want Is You, Cactus, Changer: Dear Eris, Take Me Higher, A.C.E Adventures in Wonderland, Under Cover, Under Cover: The Mad Squad, My Lover, Higher, Savage (삐딱선), HJZM: The Butterfly Phantasy, Siren: Dawn, Callin’, 5TAR (Incompletion), Cactus (선인장; Seoninjang), Favorite Boys (도깨비), and Changer.

Jun Facts

  • He was in middle school when he watched the music video of Rain’s Rainism and that inspired him to pursue a career in music. He then found a music academy and soon started to learn music.
  • Jun was 19 years old when he shifted to Seoul in order to pursue his passion for singing.
  • He has previously trained under CJ E&M and Jellyfish Entertainment. During his time with Jellyfish Entertainment, he trained alongside the boy band VIXX.
  • He was cast as Kangta’s look-alike in the television mystery music game show I Can See Your Voice.
  • Jun’s fellow A.C.E members have been Donghun, Kang Yu-chan, Wow, and Kim Byeongkwan.
  • On May 23, 2017, A.C.E debuted with the single Cactus which peaked at #24 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • A.C.E has been a recipient of many accolades including the SBS PopAsia Award, The Fact Music Award, the Soribada Best K-Music Award, the APAN Music Award, and the Global GAIA Service Award.
  • A.C.E released its extended play Under Cover on May 17, 2019, and its tracklist includes Do It Like Me, Under Cover, If You Heard, and others. It peaked at #8 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • On October 29, 2019, A.C.E released the extended play Under Cover: The Mad Squad and it peaked at #7 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • A.C.E released the extended play HJZM: The Butterfly Phantasy on September 2, 2020, and it includes songs like Baby Tonight, Clover, Golden Goose, Stand by You, and Favorite Boys. It peaked at #4 on the Gaon Album Chart.
  • On June 23, 2021, A.C.E released its extended play titled Siren: Dawn and it includes songs like Story, Higher Love, Atlantis, and others. The EP peaked at #1 on the iTunes Worldwide Albums Chart 2 days after it was released and at #2 on the Gaon Album Chart. It also reached the #1 spot in many countries including Brazil, Belarus, Ecuador, Peru, Norway, Indonesia, Russia, Singapore, Portugal, and Malaysia and NME praised the members, stating: “Keep an eye on this headstrong quintet, because there’s no question they’ll surprise us in the years to come.”
  • A.C.E has shown support for the LGBTQ+ community and their song Spark has been a part of the original soundtrack for boys’ love Korean drama Light on Me.
  • As a member of A.C.E, Jun has been a part of many television projects such as Zombie Detective, Extreme A.C.E, A.C.E Chuja Island, and Hello, My Twenties! 2.
  • Jun has also been cast in other shows like Tinted With You, I Need Romance 3, Meloholic, Big Issue, Age of Youth 2, and Persevere, Goo Hae Ra.
  • One of his closest celebrity friends has been actor and rapper Kim Min-jae.
  • He enjoys eating strawberry yogurt.
  • Jun likes soccer.
  • He enlisted for his military service as an active duty soldier on February 7, 2022.
