What is Curtis Enis' Net Worth?

Curtis Enis is an American former professional football player who has a net worth of $1 million. Curtis Enis was born in Union City, Ohio in June 1976. Enis was a running back who was a Parade All-American at Mississinawa Valley High School. He was also given Ohio's Mr. Football Award from the Associated Press. Enis then attended Pennsylvania State University where he was a consensus first-team All-American. He was involved in a minor gift acceptance scandal and was not able to play in his final bowl game.

Enis was the #5 pick of the 1998 NFL Draft by the Chicago Bears. He held out and missed training camp and two exhibition games. Enis played for the Bears from 1998 to 2000 and was injured almost as soon as he started playing. He battled through injuries and signed a one year deal with the Cleveland Browns in 2001 but was forced to retire due to the degenerative condition in his left knee. Curtis was the head football coach for Bradford High School in Bradford, Ohio from 2010 to 2013 and resigned in 2014.
