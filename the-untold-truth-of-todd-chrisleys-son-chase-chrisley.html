Who is Chase Chrisley?

Chase Chrisley was born on 1 June 1996 under the sign of Gemini, in South Carolina USA. He is a 23-year-old reality TV star, but probably best recognized for being the son of reality TV personality and millionaire Todd Chrisley and his wife Julie Chrisley. He is also known for starring in the USA Network reality series “Chrisley Knows Best”, alongside his entire family.

How rich is he, as of now? Chase Chrisley Net Worth and Assets

Chase Chrisley’s net worth is over $5.5 million, earned from being involved successfully in the entertainment industry as a part of “Chrisley Knows Best” and “Growing Up Chrisley”.

Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity, Education

Reality TV Star and “Chrisley Knows Best”

Chase developed his love for baseball at a very young age. Although he always wanted to become a professional baseball player, his charisma led him to pursue career in the world of entertainment. So, Chase started career as a reality TV personality when he appeared in the family reality TV series, entitled “Chrisley Knows Best”, which has been aired on the USA Network.

The show follows the lives of real estate mogul Todd Chrisley and his family, and premiered on 11 March 2014, completing its seventh season in November 2019. Chase has been appearing in the series since the first season, and over the time he became recognized as ‘The Favorite’. He is very close to his younger sister Savannah Chrisley and they are known as ‘partners in crime’, since they frequently create trouble. Moreover, Chase spends some time running his own podcast show, entitled “The Chase Christy Podcast’”.

“Growing Up Chrisley” and Other Shows

Furthermore, Chase and his younger sister Savannah got their own “Chrisley Knows Best” spin-off series entitled “Growing Up Chrisley”, which has been aired on the USA Network as well. It is focused on their moving from Nashville to Los Angeles, with the intention of continuing their studies at college and becoming independent. The show premiered on 2 April 2019, and is currently in its second season. Chase has also appeared in other shows, including “WWE Raw”, “Steve Harvey”, and “The Wendy Williams Show”, among others.
